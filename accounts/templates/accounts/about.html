About Our System

The Online Book Review and Recommendation System is developed as a Final Year Project for the course CS-519 at the Virtual University of Pakistan.

This platform helps users to discover, review, and share books. Users can explore a wide catalog of books, submit personal reviews and ratings, maintain a wishlist, and receive personalized recommendations based on their favorite genres.

Administrators can manage books, moderate reviews, and ensure the quality of the platform. The system aims to provide a seamless and user-friendly experience, encouraging a culture of reading and knowledge sharing.
